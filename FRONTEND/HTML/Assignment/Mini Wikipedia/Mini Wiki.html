Recap on websites and web development

1. A website is a collection of publicly accessible, interlinked web pages that share a single domain name.
2. A web page is a digital document that is displayed in a web browser and accessed over the internet, they are the building blocks of websites and also serve as a fundamental unit of information on the World Wide Web
3. Web development encompasses the processes involved in creating and maintaining websites or web applications for the internet. It involves a combination of programming, design, and content creation to produce functional and visually appealing online experiences
